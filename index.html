ERP IIIT-A (प्रज्ञानम्)
The IIITA ERP app is a cutting-edge solution designed to streamline student services and enhance user experience. With features like fee updates, mess history tracking, profile management, and medication records, the app offers a user-friendly interface for quick and efficient access to essential information.
